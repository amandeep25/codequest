Rules

  • Participants will register in teams of 3-5 people. Each team will be allotted a PC.
  • For the duration of the game, each team will be allowed to carry 1 mobile phone with an app that will be provided to them.
  • The game consists of 5 levels.
  • Each level will be divided into 3 parts.
    • A coding puzzle will be given to the participants, on solving which they will get the clue to a location within the university campus. The participants will go to the given location and find a hidden QR code.
    • On scanning the QR code, a theme based question will be revealed. The answer to this riddle will be the password to unlock the next level.
    • This password will have to be entered on the respective PC allotted to the team.
  • The team to decode all the levels first wins!
  • Each team will be given ONLY 3 ATTEMPTS for each question.
  • Out of the 3-5 members, 1-2 people should always be seated at the PC, solving the coding puzzles. Rest of the team members will go to respective locations to find the hidden QR codes.
  • Team members can interchange their position after each level.
  • All answers are single word answers, and must be entered in either small-caps or all caps.
  • participants are requested not to make use of the "enter" key and use the buttons on the website.
  • The Puzzles include various logical, coding questions, error detection, and Theme based puzzles.
  • In case of a tie, a tie-breaker will take place to determine the winner.
  • Use of internet is strictly prohibited.
  • if any team is found to be using any Unfair Means, they will be DISQUALIFIED on the spot.
